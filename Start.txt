General Info
Recycling is provided at no extra cost with your basic garbage service. There is no limit on the amount of clean recycling you can set out for collection. Put recycling that doesn’t fit in your cart in old recycling bin or cardboard boxes (limit 3’x3’x3’).Remember to clean all items, and to throw ALL lids in the trash.
